Shao-Yuan Lo   羅紹元

I am a 2nd year PhD student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Johns Hopkins University, advised by Prof. Vishal M. Patel. Previously, I received my M.S. (Electronics Engineering) and B.S. (EECS Honors Program) from National Chiao Tung University, and worked with Prof. Hsueh-Ming Hang.

My research is on computer vision and machine learning. During my master's education, I was working on efficient semantic segmentation algorithms for self-driving applications. Currently, my research is focusing on adversarial machine learning. I am exploring novel adversarial threat models and adversarially robust methods for computer vision systems.
