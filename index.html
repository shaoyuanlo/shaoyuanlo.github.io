Shao-Yuan Lo   羅紹元

I am a 4th year PhD student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Johns Hopkins University, advised by Prof. Vishal M. Patel. My research area lies in computer vision and machine learning. The research problems I have been working on include adversarial robustness, domain adaptation, semantic segmentation, depth estimation, video recognition, novelty detection, etc. I received my M.S. (Electronics Engineering) and B.S. (EECS Honors Program) degrees from National Chiao Tung University in 2019 and 2017, respectively, advised by Prof. Hsueh-Ming Hang.

I also spent a wonderful time as an Applied Scientist Intern at Amazon Astro team, working with Wei Wang, Jim Thomas and Cheng-Hao Kuo; and at Amazon Just Walk Out team, working with Poojan Oza, Sumanth Chennupati and Alejandro Galindo.
